Season opener record: 23–12

Vs Akron

8/29 W, 38-7

Replay:

UCF recovered its own fumble on the opening play. Blake Bortles then passed to Breshad Perriman for a 91-yard touchdown, the longest play from scrimmage in school history.

“Every time I touch the ball I want to score. Honestly, I thought somebody was going to tackle me from behind.” Breshad Perriman, wide receiver

At FIU

9/6 W, 38-0

Replay:

UCF holds FIU to 31 rushing yards and avenges the 2011 loss in Miami.

“You’ve got to stay on top of people and keep your foot on the gas, and I thought we did.” George O’Leary, coach

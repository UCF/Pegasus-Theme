Welcome to College Open House!
You’re invited to come early to the UCF vs. Boston College game and attend the open houses at UCF.

Students can participate in amazing race style activities at several colleges to be entered into a drawing for either a family four pack of tickets to the UCF vs. Marshall Football game on Oct. 8 or one of two $50 textbook scholarships!

Stop by the College Open House table in the Student Union between 11 AM and Noon to get a passport and map of the colleges. Use the passport to collect one-inch buttons from each participating college and return them to the College Open House staff in the Student Union by 4:00 PM to earn entries into the drawing. Winners will be notified during the week of September 12.

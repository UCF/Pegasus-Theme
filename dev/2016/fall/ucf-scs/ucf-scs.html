First Frost

Words and audio by Deanna Ferrante
Photos by Nick Leyva '15
[social_buttons]

Emerging from the ashes of a winless season, the Knights soared to victory in their 2016 opener against South Carolina State. The game marks the first success for UCF's new head coach Scott Frost, who joined Athletic Department late last year. With this win, Frost and the team's offensive players rolled up the largest amount of yards gained in a game since 2014. Not to be outdone, the black and gold defense secured the 23rd shutout by the Knights in school history.
